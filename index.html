About me

Hi, I’m Clara Youssef, originally a Biomedical Engineering graduate from Cairo University, now living in Canada and studying Artificial Intelligence at Sheridan College. My path moved from engineering into tech because I’ve always been drawn to building things that are smart, useful, and actually make life easier.

These days, I spend most of my time working on projects that bring together AI, DevOps, and full-stack development. I’ve built MLOps pipelines, dashboards that track and alert in real-time, automation systems that save teams hours of work, and web apps that are simple and intuitive to use. I love the mix of solving technical challenges while also making sure what I build feels practical and user-friendly.

What excites me most is exploring new ideas and turning them into something real. I enjoy reading research papers, especially ones that haven’t been applied yet, and challenging myself to learn or even try to implement them. I’m a very technical person at heart, always curious about what’s new in the world of AI and how it can be used in practice. Lately, I’ve been fascinated by Quantum AI and the possibilities it opens up for the future.
